# Exact defects

## Defect 1 — Dataset lanes converge prematurely

The current graphic maintains separate MIMIC-III and PhysioNet lanes only through their temporal-model boxes. Both then point to one shared normalized-export box and one shared aggregation box before splitting again into two resources.

Even though the figure says “not pooled,” this topology can imply that outputs from both datasets are jointly normalized or aggregated.

The figure must instead preserve dataset separation through:

```text
source records
→ preprocessing and canonical IDs
→ deterministic rule labels
→ four temporal-model annotations
→ normalized probability/binary exports
→ five-source aggregation
→ outcome, covariates, DAG/adjustment, and provenance
→ dataset-specific estimator-ready resource
```

Use two separate parallel paths:

```text
MIMIC-III export → MIMIC-III aggregation → MIMIC-III resource
PhysioNet export → PhysioNet aggregation → PhysioNet resource
```

The two resources may converge visually only at the downstream-characterization stage, where the method family is shared but analyses remain separate.

Do not draw any arrow that implies:

* cross-dataset voting;
* cross-dataset aggregation;
* pooled records;
* a shared causal-analysis table;
* semantic equivalence of similarly named proxies.

A compact shared label such as “same interface, separate execution” is acceptable.

## Defect 2 — Text is too small

The current script uses many labels between approximately 5.5 and 7.8 points. This is below the requested approximately 9-point final embedded size.

Revise the figure so:

* primary box text is at least approximately 9 points at final paper size;
* stage headings are at least approximately 9 points;
* secondary gate labels and the bounded LLM note are preferably at least 8.5 points and never below 8 points;
* all text remains readable in the main PDF at normal viewing scale;
* no text is clipped or overlaps arrows or boxes.

Simplify wording rather than shrinking text.

Examples of compact labels:

```text
Preprocess + IDs
Rule labels
4 model annotations
Normalize exports
5-source vote
Outcome + covariates
DAG + provenance
```

The caption can carry detail that does not fit legibly in the graphic.

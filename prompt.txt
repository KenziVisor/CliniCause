Describe the three estimators:

### CausalForestDML

Present as the primary nonlinear heterogeneous-effect estimator.

* record-level model-estimated CATE outputs;
* sample-level mean summary.

### LinearDML

Present as the main structured comparator.

Explain that it supplies a different final-stage model form while preserving the DML adjustment framework.

Its role is cross-model-form triangulation, not merely a fallback baseline.

### CausalPFN

Present as a complementary estimator from a distinct modeling paradigm.

State confidently that it is included to test whether the main directional patterns are specific to the two DML estimators.

Do not characterize its architecture, training corpus, theoretical properties, or novelty without a verified primary citation.

Where supported, explain that all three estimators produce exposure-specific conditional-effect summaries over the analyzed records.

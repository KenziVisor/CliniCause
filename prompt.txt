# 8. Permitted repairs

Make no source change unless a visible defect is confirmed.

Permitted repairs are limited to:

* local line breaks;
* `\allowbreak`;
* scoped table-column widths;
* scoped `\tabcolsep`;
* scoped `\arraystretch`;
* local font sizing inside one table;
* nonbreaking spaces;
* safe URL or identifier breaking;
* local caption or float-placement correction;
* local Hebrew/Latin direction formatting.

Do not change:

* prose meaning;
* scientific claims;
* numbers;
* signs;
* rounding;
* citations;
* labels;
* table values;
* table row order;
* figure files;
* figure captions;
* figure placement;
* estimator terminology;
* evidence status;
* causal or clinical strength.

Do not apply global `\sloppy`, global `\emergencystretch`, smaller margins, reduced line spacing, or global font-size suppression.
